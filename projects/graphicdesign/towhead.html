Towhead

Promotional materials and visual assets developed for Towhead. The project focused on creating eye-catching event graphics and marketing materials that could function consistently across print and digital formats.

Role

Graphic Designer

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Towhead is an Oakland-based band that commissioned a series of promotional flyers and posters for an upcoming performance in Portland, Oregon.


This project involved the creation of promotional materials supporting Towhead's public-facing events and programming. The objective was to develop visuals that felt bold, memorable, and adaptable across multiple formats while maintaining a clear and cohesive identity.
