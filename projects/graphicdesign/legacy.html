Legacy Game Store

Legacy Game Store is an independent gaming retailer based in Webster, New York. During the store's relocation and expansion, I developed promotional graphics, print materials, and merchandise designs that supported marketing efforts and community engagement. The project focused on creating bold, recognizable visuals that communicated important updates while reflecting the personality and culture of the store.

Role

Graphic Designer

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop

Legacy Game Store serves a diverse community of tabletop, trading card, and video game enthusiasts. As part of the store's transition to a new location, a variety of promotional and branded materials were developed to help communicate the move and maintain visibility within the local gaming community.


The project included posters, flyers, marketing graphics, and apparel concepts designed for both physical and digital distribution. Each piece was created to be visually engaging, easy to understand, and consistent with the store's identity.


Working across multiple formats required balancing clear communication with strong visual impact. The resulting materials helped promote events, inform customers, and create a cohesive brand presence during an important period of growth for the business.

Promotional materials were developed to support the store's relocation, special events, and community outreach efforts. Designs were created for both print and digital distribution, helping communicate important information while maintaining a cohesive visual identity.

In addition to promotional graphics, custom apparel concepts were developed for Legacy Game Store. The designs were intended to function both as merchandise and as extensions of the store's visual identity, incorporating gaming culture, community references, and brand elements.
