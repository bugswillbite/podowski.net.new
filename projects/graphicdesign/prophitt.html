The Prophitt

The Prophitt Artist Identity is an ongoing visual branding and creative direction project developed for Rochester-based producer and DJ The Prophitt. The project encompasses logo design, album artwork, promotional materials, digital content, merchandise concepts, and web design, creating a cohesive visual system that supports the artist's evolving musical output.

Role

Creative Director, Designer & Developer

Tools

Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, After Effects, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

prophitt.net

My relationship with the Prophitt is an ongoing collaboration with the independent musician, encompassing visual identity development, album artwork, merchandise deign, promotional campaigns, video editing, and social media content. Working closely with the artist, I help translate evolving musical themes and creative concepts into a cohesive visual language that spans digital and physical formats.


My role includes designing artwork for releases, developing promotional materials, producing music videos and short-form video content, and creating assets for streaming platforms, social media, live performances, and merchandise. The project requires balancing experimentation and consistency while maintaining a recognizable visual identity actoss multiple releases.

The visual identity for The Prophitt is built around a flexible system capable of adapting to different releases while maintaining a consistent artistic voice. Through typography, image treatment, color palettes, and graphic motifs, each campaign is designed to feel distinct while remaining connected to the broader identity of the project.


Across album rollouts, merchandise launches, and promotional campaigns, I develop assets that support audience engagement and storytelling wihle ensuring visual consistency across digital platforms, physical products, and live events. This process often involves rapic iteration, collaborative feedback, and adapting creative solutions to evolving project goals and timelines.

Artwork was developed for multiple releases, including Work & Progress, 4 UR CONSIDERATION, and Zero Cool. Each project required a unique visual language informed by the themes, atmosphere, and sonic character of the music. Designs combined photography, digital manipulation, typography, and illustration techniques to create memorable visual experiences that complemented the listening experience while maintaining consistency within the broader artist identity.

In addition to release artwork, a wide range of promotional assets were developed to support audience engagement and marketing campaigns. This included social media graphics, event posters, announcement visuals, merchandise concepts, digital advertisements, and promotional content used across streaming platforms, social media, and live events. Each piece was designed to strengthen the artist's visual identity while remaining adaptable to different formats and audiences, helping create a cohesive presence across both digital and physical environments.

A dedicated website was designed and developed to serve as a central hub for music releases, discography, events, streaming links, and artist information. The site incorporates custom navigation, searchable discography functionality, responsive layouts, and interactive design elements tailored to the artist's visual identity.

Video content was produced to support music releases, social media promotion, and artist storytelling. Projects ranged from short-form promotional edits and visualizers to full music videos, combining motion graphics, editing, typography, and visual effects to extend the artist's identity across video platforms.
