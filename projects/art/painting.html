Painting & Drawing

Painting and drawing serve as the foundation of my artistic practice, providing a space to explore observation, narrative, experimentation, and personal expression. Working across traditional and digital media, these pieces investigate themes of memory, identity, surrealism, and visual storytelling through color, composition, and form.


The collection includes sketches, finished illustrations, paintings, mixed-media works, and conceptual explorations developed throughout both academic and independent practice. Together, these works reflect an ongoing interest in translating ideas, emotions, and experiences into visual language.
