3D Modelling

3D modeling extends my interest in visual design into digital space, combining artistic experimentation with technical production workflows. Projects include objects, environments, characters, and conceptual forms created for animation, visualization, interactive media, and game development.


Using digital sculpting, modeling, texturing, and rendering techniques, these works explore the possibilities of virtual space while maintaining a strong focus on composition, detail, and visual storytelling. The collection reflects both artistic exploration and an interest in creating assets that function within broader digital experiences.

Tools

Maya, Womp, Blender, Unity
