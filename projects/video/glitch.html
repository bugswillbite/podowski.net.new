Glitch Art & Short-Form

My "Glitch Art" explores the aesthetic possibilities of technological failure, distortion, and digital corruption. Using techniques such as datamoshing, compression, signal interference, and manipulated digital processes, these projects embrace errors as creative tools rather than imperfections. The resulting works challenge expectations of image fidelity while revealing the hidden structures and vulnerabilities of digital media.

Tools

Premiere Pro, Resolume, Sandin Image Processor, After Effects, EbSynth
