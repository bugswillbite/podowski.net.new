Experimental Film

My experimental film work explores visual storytelling beyond traditional narrative structures. Through abstraction, montage, sound design, and unconventional editing techniques, these projects investigate perception, memory, emotion, and the relationship between moving images and time. Each film serves as a creative experiment, using video as a medium for exploration, discovery, and artistic expression.
