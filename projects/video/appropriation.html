Appropriation

My appropriation work is an experimental video method exploring remix culture, found footage, media transformation, and the reinterpretation of existing visual material. The work combines editing, collage, motion, sound design, and archival imagery to create new narratives from familiar sources.


This body of work investigates the ways images gain new meaning through reuse, juxtaposition, and digital manipulation. Drawing from archival media, internet culture, and popular imagery, these videos explore memory, authorship, technology, and contemporary visual language.
