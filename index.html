ABOUT ME

I'm Izabella "Bella" Podowski, a Product Designer, Creative Developer, and Multimedia Creator based in Rochester, New York.

I design digital experiences that combine UX/UI design, frontend development, graphic design, motion, and storytelling to create intuitive, engaging, and visually distinctive products.

With a Bachelor of Fine Arts from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, I've collaborated with musicians, independent businesses, and community organizations to create work spanning web, branding, interactive media, print, video, and game design.

I'm especially interested in projects centered around music, culture, and visual identity - balancing thoughtful user experiences with bold creative direction and technical implementation.
